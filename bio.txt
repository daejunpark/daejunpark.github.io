Daejun Park is a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at UIUC. His research interests are in programming languages and formal methods. He received his B.S. and M.S. degrees in Computer Science and Engineering from Seoul National University in 2006 and 2008, respectively. He had served as a founding member and a technical lead in a startup that develops static program analysis tools for C/C++ and Java, which had been acquired by Fasoo in 2010. He is a previous recipient of Korean Government Scholarship for his PhD study, and won a Distinguished Paper Award at OOPSLA 2016 for his paper, 'Semantics-Based Program Verifiers for All Languages.'
