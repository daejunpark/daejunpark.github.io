Daejun Park is a Formal Verification Lead at Runtime Verification, Inc., where he is working on formal verification of smart contracts and consensus protocols. He is broadly interested in applying formal methods to real-world systems and applications. He has worked on language-parametric program verification, translation validation of production compilers, and verifiable computing for approximate computation. He had also served as a founding member and a tech lead in Sparrow, Ltd., a startup that develops static program analysis tools for embedded system software. He received a Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, and his B.S. and M.S. degrees from Seoul National University. His work on language-parametric program verification received a distinguished paper award at OOPSLA'16.
